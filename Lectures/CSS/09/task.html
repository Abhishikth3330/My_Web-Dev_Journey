Famous Places in Nagpur

Deekshabhoomi

Deekshabhoomi is a sacred monument of Buddhism located in Nagpur. It is a place where Dr. B. R. Ambedkar converted to Buddhism along with his followers. The large Stupa is a symbol of peace and equality.

Zero Mile Stone

The Zero Mile Stone is a historical monument marking the geographical center of colonial India. It is an iconic spot with four horses and a sandstone pillar that reflects Nagpur's rich history.

Futala Lake

Futala Lake is one of Nagpur's most beautiful and serene locations. Known for its colorful fountains and ambient lighting, it's a perfect spot for evening strolls and leisure.
